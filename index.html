JUSTIN MITCHELL

Justin’s investment approach prioritizes liquidity, high-quality operators, and capital preservation, establishing him as a world-class real estate lender and operator.

Before founding RWA Group, Justin was a co-founder at Quanta Finance, a top 5 national private credit mortgage lender in the US and UK with over 3,500 loans originated for a total loan volume of $2.5B+, and zero loss of principal capital.

Previously, Justin was VP of Analytics (Employee #5) at Genesis Capital, a high-growth specialty finance company that made bridge loans to residential real estate developers. Genesis Capital was backed by Oaktree Capital Management, one of the world's premier money managers focused on lending and real estate markets, and was acquired by Goldman Sachs.

Justin started his Real Estate career in 2007 at Countrywide as an analyst on the Secondary Markets team. After Bank of America acquired Countrywide, Justin was a senior analyst on BofA’s Credit Risk Management team, which he helped grow from a small team of 25 to over 3,000 headcount post-GFC.

KURT DARADICS

Prior to co-founding RWA Group, Kurt has honed his craft of go-to-market, business development, and team building with high-level executive and leadership experience at tech companies including Esri, Lime, Hunt Club, as well as being a founder (CitySourced, acquired in a Vista backed PE roll up).

Kurt has experienced the continuum of problem-solution fit, product-market fit, growth, and scale. Kurt has a reputation for helping organizations make positive commercial and social impacts in the world.

Prior to a 15 year career in tech, Kurt started his career with five years in the real estate sector as a mortgage broker, until he transitioned to tech in 2008.

Kurt studied Mass Communication at UCSB and has a CA Real Estate License

ELIAS BADIN

Prior to joining RWA Group, Elias Badin was the Vice President, Finance at Transom Capital, a mid market Private Equity Firm, where Elias partnered with the executive leadership of Transom’s portfolio companies on finance-related activities, including financial reporting and compliance, cash flow management, and the successful execution of long-term strategic initiatives designed to generate enterprise value by delivering cross-functional operating efficiencies.

Previously, Elias was a consultant at NOW CFO, where he advised and supported companies with limited accounting and finance functions by providing strategic leadership, subject matter expertise and transactional support. Elias also served as Director of Accounting for FTD Companies’ ProFlowers division. Elias started his career in accounting at PwC where he spent 5 years in the alternative investment practice consulting hedge funds and private equity clients in the assurance practice.

Elias holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and Business Administration from the University of San Diego and is a former CPA.

INVESTORS

Features and Benefits

INSTITUTIONAL ACCESS

We source large volumes of institutional grade real estate that offers attractive risk-adjusted yields. Sponsor focused lending philosophy. Emphasis on liquidity, experience, creditworthiness, and net worth.

FIXED INCOME

We invest in diversified, liquid assets in strong tier-one metros. Short-term loans to reduce market and duration risk. Secured first trust deeds. Quarterly interest distributions to investors.

DEFENSIVE ASSET CLASS

We focus on hard real estate assets rooted by strong credit and underwriting principles Responsible Loan to Value (LTV) Rigorous collateral monitoring and control of loan disbursements. Prudent use of leverage, used to minimize “cash drag” and maximize investor returns. Focus on experienced sponsors and operators with proven track records and financial strength

DIVERSIFICATION

Real estate debt returns are largely uncorrelated to mainstream asset classes allowing diversification in a multi-asset portfolio.
